Instructions

1. Player 1: Use 'W' and 'S' to move your paddle up and down.

2. Player 1: Use 'D' to shoot blocks.

3. Player 1: Use 'A' to rotate blocks.

1. Player 2: Use the up and down arrow keys to move your paddle up and down.

2. Player 2: Use the left arrow key to shoot blocks.

3. Player 2: Use the right arrow key to rotate blocks.
